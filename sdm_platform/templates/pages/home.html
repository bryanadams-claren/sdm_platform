Perspicacious Health

Shared Decision Making (SDM) puts patients in control of their own healthcare. Perspicacious Health helps patient, providers and payers work together to replace prior authorization with educated consensus.

About

Our clinical staff works directly with patient who are at a treatment crossroads. Medical literature can be dense and confusing, and active providers often can't spare the time to help patients sort through the latest data. Perspicacious Health steps in and fills this gap with access to the best information, presented in easy-to-understand ways, using modern synchronous and asynchronous communication platforms. We help patients understand their treatment options and, in so doing, make sure everyone gets the right care at the right time.

Who We Serve

Patients

We help patients learn about treatment options at their own pace, from industry-leading sources, so they can make the right choices for themselves.

Providers

We help sort through the medical literature and provide patients with the time and space they need to get their questions answered and make a confident decision.

Health Plans

By replacing Prior Authorization with SDM, plans create better member experiences, reduce overhead expense, and still see med expense reductions.
